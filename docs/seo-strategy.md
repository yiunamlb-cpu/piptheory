_Last updated: 2026-05-30. Internal reference (not published to the site)._

Grounded in live-SERP research of the currency-strength niche. Re-validate quarterly — the
"best … 2026" terms refresh yearly and competitor positions move.

## TL;DR — the thesis

The head terms ("currency strength meter", "free currency strength meter", "forex heatmap")
are **owned by high-authority, price-based, real-time tools** (MarketMilk/BabyPips, OANDA,
Investing.com, TradingView, Myfxbook). We can't out-rank those for ~12+ months, and we can't
out-"real-time" a 10-second ticker when we refresh every 4h.

**The empty lane = _free + macro/fundamentals_.** Every free tool is price-only; the one
fundamentals competitor (LogikFx) **paywalls it**. So we compete on:

1. **Macro / fundamental** intent (only LogikFx, and it's paid)
2. **Free / no-signup** (rivals gate behind paywalls, broker accounts, or MT4/MT5 downloads)
3. **Non-USD cross pairs** (the ~20 crosses — weakest SERPs; programmatic via our 28 pair pages)
4. **Explainer cluster** tied to our differentiator (fundamentals, COT, REER, carry, etc.)

Cede "live/real-time" head terms. Own "macro", "fundamental", "free/no-signup", and the
long-tail cross-pair + minor-currency queries.

## Link / distribution tactics (realistic for a new low-authority site)

1. **Embeddable widget** — a small "currency strength" embed with a `?source=` backlink. The
   single highest-leverage link engine; how these tools earn natural links.
2. **Get into "best currency strength meter" roundups** — pitch ChartSnipe,
   currencystrengthindicator.net, Switch Markets, Medium listicles; lead with "free + fundamentals".
3. **Primary-source link bait** — a clean COT-positioning dashboard + REER fair-value table
   (repackage CFTC/BIS data better than the originals).
4. **Tool directories / forums (no spam)** — BabyPips-style resource lists, Forex Factory
   beginner-resources thread, genuine answers on r/Forex / r/Daytrading (tool as supporting evidence).
5. **Google Discover** — Tier-3 newsy pieces with original charts + clear author byline + fast
   load (Cloudflare Pages helps).
6. **Internal linking** — all 36 tool pages ↔ matching explainers; concentrates topical authority.
7. **Citation-style outreach** — email explainers as "plain-English version of [paper]" to
   finance educators/newsletters (low-ask, high-credibility links).
