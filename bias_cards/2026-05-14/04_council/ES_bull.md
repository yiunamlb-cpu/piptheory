The CPI print changes the balance of risks from “Fed might hike” to “Fed is still in your corner,” which is a regime shift that historically supports dip‑buying. We accept that the trade must be held with a hard invalidation at 5800, and size must reflect the fragility the contrarian identifies. But until that invalidation is triggered, the bull case is the path of least resistance.

CONFIDENCE IN THIS BULL CASE: 6/10
