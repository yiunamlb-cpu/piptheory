A single stock disappointment alone is not invalidation; it requires a systemic retreat from the AI capex theme.

CONTRARIAN ENGAGEMENT:
  Strongest contrarian point against this bull case: “Narrative fragility and concentration risk — because NQ is so heavily exposed to AI capex, any crack (capex miss, export restriction, or recession scare hitting tech spending) causes a far sharper repricing than in the broad index. The contrarian’s 30% probability of an 5‑8% slide is acknowledged, but that slide itself would be the dip to buy, not the structural top. The bull case survives as long as no major player publicly retreats from the AI buildout — and nothing in the current macro data suggests that retreat is imminent.

CONFIDENCE IN THIS BULL CASE: 7/10
  Note: The case strength rests on AI capex remaining unblemished; the macro overhang is a headwind but not a disproof. This is a strong thematic argument, not a tactical oversold bounce.
