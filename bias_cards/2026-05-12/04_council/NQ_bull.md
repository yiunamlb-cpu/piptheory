CONTRARIAN ENGAGEMENT:

Rebuttal: The contrarian’s argument is valid *as a risk*, but it depends on a specific data print and an assumption that AI‑driven earnings cannot offset multiple contraction. Moreover, the real‑yield spike from an energy shock is likely transitory — demand destruction and China’s deflationary impulse will cap long‑dated inflation expectations, limiting how high real yields can sustainably rise. The bull case survives the challenge unless the CPI print *and* the Fed’s reaction push real yields to a new regime (>2.0%). The 70% probability that the contrarian does *not* win in the next 2–4 weeks is already embedded in the consensus, and the resulting asymmetry (small downside, larger upside) favours a long entry.

CONFIDENCE IN THIS BULL CASE: **6/10**
