Macro Currency Strength Meter

The Macro Currency Strength Meter is a 100% free, systematic gauge of how strong or weak each major currency is right now. It mechanically synthesises five macro pillars — interest-rate differentials, economic growth, institutional positioning, risk sentiment and commodity terms-of-trade — each measured against its own history and ranked across the eight majors. Updated free every day, it reflects the slow macro tide rather than intraday noise — an objective, jargon-free read on which currencies are fundamentally strong or weak.
