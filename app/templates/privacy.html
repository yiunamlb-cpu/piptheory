Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

PIPTHEORY ("we", "the site") publishes free, systematic macro research at piptheory.com. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit and how cookies, advertising and analytics are used.

Information we collect

The site is read-only and does not require accounts or sign-ups. We do not ask for or store personal information such as your name, address or payment details. Standard, non-identifying technical data (such as browser type, approximate region and pages viewed) may be processed by the third-party services below.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We and our partners use them to remember your preferences (for example, light or dark mode) and to support advertising and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features (like your theme choice) may not persist if you do.

Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit the site. These companies may use cookies (including Google's advertising cookies) and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate, how the site is used (such as which pages are popular). This data is anonymised and is not used to identify individual visitors.

Your choices (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct or request deletion of data held about you, and to object to certain processing. Because we do not collect identifying personal information directly, most requests should be directed to the third-party services above. You can also control cookies through your browser and the opt-out links provided.

Disclaimer

All content is educational macro context only and is not investment advice. Nothing on this site is a recommendation to buy or sell any currency or financial instrument.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@piptheory.com.
