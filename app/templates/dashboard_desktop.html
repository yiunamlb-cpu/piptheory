How to read this page
  1. Regime below classifies today's macro mix (growth vs inflation). It sets the stage.
  2. Today's read is the morning brief — a 90-second narrative of what the data says.
  3. Today's calls are the few instruments where conviction is ≥ 7 of 10. Most days the list is short or empty — that's the point.
  4. Driving themes are the macro forces underneath. All instruments at the bottom is the full table.
